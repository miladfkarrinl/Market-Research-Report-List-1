Sodium Salt laxatives, such as sodium phosphate, draw water into the intestines, promoting bowel movements. Magnesium Salt laxatives, including magnesium hydroxide, work by osmotically attracting water, increasing stool bulk and frequency. The "Other" category encompasses a variety of less common saline agents, combining different minerals or compounds to achieve similar laxative effects. Each type offers unique properties and targeted uses in constipation relief.</p></p>
