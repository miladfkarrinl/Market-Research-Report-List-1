<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Life Support Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Venoarterial (VA) ECMO</li><li>Venovenous (VV) ECMO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extracorporeal Life Support Devices (ECLS) market primarily includes two types: Venoarterial (VA) ECMO and Venovenous (VV) ECMO. VA ECMO is used to support both cardiac and pulmonary function by providing oxygenated blood directly to the arterial system, ideal for patients with severe cardiac failure. In contrast, VV ECMO primarily addresses pulmonary issues by facilitating gas exchange while maintaining cardiac output, making it suitable for those with severe respiratory failure. Both systems enhance patient survival in critical conditions.</p></p>
